Equivalence System

The idea of the project arose to keep pace with the increasing technical development, to exploit the widespread communication media and the evolution of web application development environments, and to take advantage of the digital infrastructure to save time, effort and money to evaluate the marks between to universities. The site specializes in evaluate student's marks when they move between tow university however, they were in public or private university. In our project, three entities were linked: student, old university, and new university, so the student could get a general evaluation for his marks. Thus, a database was provided for subjects and students. You can also add a new subject if they were not found. In addition to you can print the result after you finish.
